Iconic monuments

India is home to some of the most awe-inspiring landmarks in the world. These monumets reflect its rich cultural heritage and historical significant:

  • Taj Mahal :Located in Agra, this symbol of eternal love is the Seven Wonder of the world.
  • Red Fort :A UNESCO World heritage site in Delhi, showcasing Mughal architecture.
  • Hawa Mahal :Known as the "Palac of winds," this jaipur marvel is Famous for its intricate latticework.
  • Charminar :An unique mosque in Hydrabad and a masterpiece of Indo-Islamic architecture.
